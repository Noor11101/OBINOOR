Privacy Policy

At OBINOOR, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and usage data collected through cookies and analytics tools.

  • Custom Order Data: Details like material preferences for custom-made bag orders.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your orders.

  • To personalize your shopping experience and offer customized products.

  • To send transactional and promotional emails if you opt-in.

  • To analyze website usage and improve our services.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

  • We use cookies to remember your preferences, keep track of your cart, and analyze website performance.

  • You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may limit your use of certain features.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party providers for payment processing, shipping, and analytics. These parties only access necessary data to perform their services and are required to protect your information.

How We Protect Your Information

  • We use encryption for sensitive data, such as payment details, and secure connections (HTTPS) across the website.

  • Access to your personal data is restricted to authorized personnel and third-party service providers.

Data Retention

We retain your data as long as needed to provide our services or as required by law. You may request the deletion of your data by contacting us.
